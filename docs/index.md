## Foreword

Welcome to Computer Conversations: a hands-on exploration of one of the most transformative shifts in how we work. We're thrilled you're here. This lab guide represents months of collaboration from our EMEA Solutions Engineering team, and it's been designed with you in mind—to give you practical, real-world experience with AI agents that are reshaping enterprise technology. Thank you for making the time to be part of this journey. What you'll learn over the coming hours isn't just about technology; it's about reimagining what's possible when human intelligence and artificial intelligence work in genuine partnership.

An agentic AI teammate is powerful because it fundamentally changes the relationship between intention and execution. Unlike traditional tools that require constant direction, AI agents operate with autonomy, contextual awareness, and the ability to learn from interaction. They don't just respond to commands—they anticipate needs, reason through complex problems, and take deliberate action on your behalf. In practical terms, this means your team can focus on strategy, creativity, and judgment while AI handles the work that's repetitive, information-intensive, or time-sensitive. The result is a multiplier effect: your most talented people become more effective, your organizations move faster, and you can tackle problems that previously seemed out of reach.

The future of work isn't about humans being replaced by machines—it's about teams being amplified by them. We're moving toward a world where the best problem-solvers won't be individuals or even teams working in isolation, but rather humans and AI agents collaborating as true teammates. That future begins with understanding how to build trust with these systems, knowing when to delegate and when to intervene, and recognizing that the most valuable skill you can develop is the ability to think clearly about what you want to achieve and why. The labs ahead are your first step into that future.

*Martijn Bosschaart*  
Solutions Engineering Manager, EMEA
