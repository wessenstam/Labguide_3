## Foreword

Welcome to the **third part of** Computer Conversations: a hands-on exploration of one of the most transformative shifts in how we work. We're thrilled you're here. This lab guide represents months of collaboration from our EMEA Solutions Engineering team, and it's been designed with you in mind—to give you practical, real-world experience with AI agents that are reshaping enterprise technology. Thank you for making the time to be part of this journey. What you'll learn over the coming hours isn't just about technology; it's about reimagining what's possible when human intelligence and artificial intelligence work in genuine partnership.

In this part of the trilogy you are going to create two new agents, that are responsible for IT Support and HR related questions. Before we are able to ask question to one of these two sepecific agents, a categorization agent will be diverting the questions from the human to the correct agent by using a workflow. In the workflow we will also be using an HTTP/REST API call to get the needed information from the conversation.
